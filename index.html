Short-Term Goals:

As of this moment, I intend to complete my master's degree in cartography and GIS at the University of Wisconsin in Madison in August this year. Following that I would like to find some temporary GIS jobs in a variety of categories and places to help familiarize myself with the GIS work environment. Beyond this, I have plans to spend 1-2 years teaching English abroad in Japan to challenge myself, become a more global citizen, and place the energy I have during my youth towards something greater than myself.


Long-Term Aspirations:

The future I envision for myself at the moment is one where I begin my cartography career building skills in the workplace that will help me create maps that have a positive effect on the world around me. However, once I achieve that success, I intend to give back what I have learned over the years to the generations below me by becoming a teacher of geography and history.


Values:

One value I truly try to uphold in my daily life is dynamic introspection. Born out of my practice of meditation when I was a Black Belt student and instructor in Tae Kwon Do, I have always set aside time to critique my flaws and improve my talents. I find this to be a way that grounds me so that I never find myself either blinded by pride or drowning in self-resentment.
